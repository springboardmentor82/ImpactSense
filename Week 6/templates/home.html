About the project

This project predicts seismic impact levels based on earthquake parameters.

About Earthquake

Magnitude, Depth, and Intensity help determine earthquake impact severity.

Parameters

Magnitude, Depth, CDI , MMI, Significance, Alert

Understanding the Parameters

Magnitude🌐 - Measures the energy released during an earthquake.

Depth⬇️ - How deep the earthquake occurs below the surface.

MMI📊 - Indicates how strongly the earthquake is felt.

CDI🧑‍🤝‍🧑 - Based on people's reported experiences.

Significance⭐ - Overall impact score of the earthquake.

Alert Level🚨 - Shows severity (Green to Red).
