About ImpactSense

A smart and reliable system designed to simplify earthquake impact analysis for everyone.

ImpactSense is an intelligent and user-friendly platform that helps people understand how dangerous an earthquake can be. Earthquake data usually contains complex numbers and scientific terms, which are difficult for most people to interpret. This system transforms that complex information into simple and meaningful results that anyone can easily understand.

How to Use

Enter the earthquake details such as magnitude, depth, and intensity values. Then click on "Predict Impact" to instantly see how severe the earthquake might be.

Understanding the Inputs

  • Magnitude – Represents the strength of the earthquake
  • Depth – Indicates how deep the earthquake occurs underground
  • MMI – Shows how strongly people feel the shaking
  • CDI – Based on how people experience and report the earthquake
  • SIG – Overall importance and impact level of the earthquake

What the Result Means

  • 🟢 Low Impact (Safe Zone) – Minimal or no danger
  • 🟡 Moderate Impact (Warning Zone) – Stay cautious
  • 🔴 High Impact (Danger Zone) – High risk, take safety measures

Why This App is Useful

  • Converts complex data into simple insights
  • Helps users quickly understand earthquake risk
  • Designed for everyone, including non-technical users
  • Promotes awareness and safety

Our goal is to make earthquake information simple, clear, and helpful for everyone.
