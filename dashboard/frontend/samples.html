FEATURE REFERENCE

Model Features

MAGNITUDE
SEISMIC ENERGY RELEASE
Logarithmic measure of energy at the earthquake source. Each unit = ~10× amplitude, ~31× energy. Primary predictor in the model.
WEIGHT: CRITICAL
MMI
MODIFIED MERCALLI INTENSITY
Instrument-calibrated shaking intensity for local geology. Strongest class discriminator — varies by soil type and epicentral distance.
WEIGHT: VERY HIGH
CDI
COMMUNITY DECIMAL INTENSITY
Human-reported shaking intensity from felt reports. Captures real perceived impact — the human dimension of seismic damage assessment.
WEIGHT: HIGH
SIGNIFICANCE
COMPOSITE IMPACT SCORE (SIG)
USGS composite metric combining magnitude, intensity and reported effects. High SIG strongly correlates with elevated alert designations.
WEIGHT: HIGH
DEPTH
HYPOCENTER DEPTH (km)
Distance from Earth's surface to the rupture point. Shallow events (<70km) cause significantly more surface damage than deep-focus earthquakes.
WEIGHT: MEDIUM
